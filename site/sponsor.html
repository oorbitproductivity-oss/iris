Why sponsor

Three honest reasons.

Indie tools need oxygen.

Iris Code is built by one person, after hours, without VC money. A few dozen sponsors at any tier turns "a hobby" into "a project I can keep showing up for."

Stays free, stays MIT.

Sponsorship is the alternative to a paid tier or a closed-source fork. The app you install today is the same one your grandchild's grandchild can fork — sponsored development keeps it that way.

You shape what ships.

Sustainer-tier sponsors and above get a vote on the next quarter's roadmap. The features other developers actually want — your call.

Thank you.

Every contribution — code, docs, sponsorship, or just a kind issue comment — keeps the project alive.
