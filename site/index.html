What it does

One orchestrator. N agents. Zero context‑switching.

Iris is the messenger goddess of Greek mythology — the god of sub‑messaging — and an acronym: Intelligent Routing for Integrated Sub‑agents. Claude Code, turned into a coordinated team — a calm dashboard where the right thing happens in the right pane at the right time.

I

Intelligent

Iris is your single point of conversation. Describe what you want, and she splits the work into discrete sub‑tasks, then suggests next actions you confirm with a click.

R

Routing

Every turn lands in the right place — the right model, the right agent, the right working directory — without you breaking flow to choose.

I

Integrated

A global Spotlight overlay, fuzzy command palette, prompt snippets, and tasteful system notifications — one surface for every keystroke you'd otherwise lose.

S

Sub‑agents

Spin up N independent claude processes — each in its own working directory or sandbox copy. Watch them stream side by side without stepping on each other.

Highlights

Built like a dashboard for the calm operator.

Every surface is keyboard‑first, every state is observable, every detail is intentional.

Spotlight

Ask Iris from anywhere.

Press Ctrl + Shift + Space from any application to summon a translucent overlay. Type your question, hit return, and your answer streams back in‑place. No window switching. No focus tax.
